Easy Tortilla Pizza

Description

Oh god. I didn't look at the recipe on this page and I hope it's not a long one. It looks super average though.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Oil
  • Seasonings
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil, season, and bake.
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings. Sprinkle it with cheese.
  3. Bake again until the cheese is melted, then season it with oregano.
